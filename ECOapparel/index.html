WHAT IS ECOAPPAREL?

This is what we do

ECOAPPAREL is an educational and motivational platform aimed to encourage shoppers to make sustainable decisions during clothing purchases that has not been manufactured by damaging the environment. It is an award based website that provides users the chance to be rewarded discouts, coupons and upgraded membership statuses all depending on their contribution.

Fashion and climate change?

Fashion is one of the biggest industries as the domestic market value globally comes to 406 billion dollars. It is no doubt that at least every one of us have purchased clothing, but oblivious to the fact that we have possibly been contributing to a global warming crisis. Our app will be useful in informing all age-groups and with such important knowledge, our users will be able to conclude with the right eco-friendly decisions.

Our Inspiration

It all started when one of our group members had watched a documentary about the impacts of fashion on our world and was completely devastated. She realized she was a contributor to a major crisis and had made all efforts to make sustainable choices. Therefore, as a group we realize that this issue should not be held a secret and enlighten many more to help out!
